Viewing Campaigns in Draft Status

Viewing campaigns in the DRAFT status enables you to edit campaign details based on the requirement or delete it and create a new campaign.
  1. In the Home page, click Home, and then click All Campaigns.
    The All Campaigns page is displayed.
  2. In the Your campaigns section, select the appropriate campaign in DRAFT status, and click View.
    The Create a New Campaign page is displayed.
  3. Update the information as per your requirement.
    For more information, refer to Creating a Campaign.
