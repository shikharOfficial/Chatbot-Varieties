Responding to Comments

You can respond to a general public comment.
  1. In the Home page, click Home, and then click All Campaigns.
    The All Campaigns page is displayed.
  2. In the Your Campaigns section, select the appropriate campaign in ACCEPTING COMMENTS status.
  3. Click View.
    The Campaign Details page is displayed.
  4. Select the COMMENTS tab.
    The Comments section displays the list of all the comments.
  5. To respond to a single comment, perform the following steps:
    1. Select the appropriate comment and click Actions, and then click Reply.
      The comment is displayed with option to respond.
    2. Click Reply.
      The box to enter your response is displayed.
    3. Enter your response in the box and click Send.
      The response is sent successfully.
  6. To respond to multiple comments, perform the following steps:
    1. Select all the appropriate comments from the list and click Actions, and then click Reply.
      The field to enter response is displayed.
    2. Enter your response in the box and click Send.
      Note: You are notified through the Notifications icon whenever any response is received from the general public.
